Get Started



Locate

Dreamcatch Academy partners with local schools to host classes and workshops on campus. Students feel right at home and parents have one less pickup. Can't find your school on our list? Join a class remotely!


Explore

We offer classes and workshops in 5 core subjects: Web Development, Design, Data, Marketing, and Business. These subjects span across various industries. Not ready to commit to a full time course? Our workshops are a great way to get started.


Enroll

Once you find the right class or workshop, enrolling is easy. Speak with one of our admission officers, sit in on a class, and start a class during one of our scheduled sessions. Don't wait, prepare your child for the future ahead with skills they won't learn in school.

Web Development

During this course, students will learn programming fundamentals, product development, Front End Development (using Javascript, HTML, and CSS), and Backend Development using the latest web language and MVC framework. Students will work on group projects during the course of the class and create a web application as the final assignment.

Design

During this course, students will learn the fundamentals of User Experience design. Students will learn how to conduct User Research and master concepts in interaction and interface design. Students will use the most common tools to prototype and test designs. At the end of class, students will work on a real client project with a team of classmates.

Data

During this course, students will learn how to master the art of analytics. Students will begin by learning the fundamentals of Excel. They will manipulate data, manage large data sets, master shortcuts, and create charts. The second half of the course will focus on using data to storytell and convey meaningful findings. Lastly, students will receive an introduction to databases and SQL.
