Locate

Dreamcatch Academy partners with local schools to host classes and workshops directly on campus. Parents don't have to worry about dropping their kids off and students feel right at home. Can't find your school on our list? Join a class remotely!


Explore

We offer classes and workshops in 5 core subjects: Web Development, Design, Data, Marketing, and Business. These subjects span across various industries. Not ready to commit to a full time course? Our workshops are a great way to get started.


Enroll

Once you find the right class or workshop, enrolling is easy. Speak with one of our admissions officers, sit in on a class, and start a class during one of our scheduled sessions. Don't wait, prepare your child for the future ahead with skills they won't learn in school.

Web Development

During this course, students will learn programming fundamentals, product development, Front End Development (using Javascript, HTML, and CSS), and Backend Development using the latest web language and MVC framework. Students will work on group projects during the course of the class and create a web application as the final assignment.

Design

During this course, students will learn the fundamentals of User Experience design. Students will learn how to conduct User Research and concepts in interaction and interface design. Students will use the most common tools to prototype and test designs. At the end of class, students will work with on a real client project with a team of classmates.
